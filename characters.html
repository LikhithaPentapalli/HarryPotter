Characters

Harry Potter:

The protagonist with a lightning bolt scar, known for his bravery and loyalty.Orphaned as a baby, he discovers he is a wizard on his eleventh birthday. Attends Hogwarts and faces numerous challenges, including battling Voldemort.

Hermione Granger:

Harry’s intelligent and hardworking friend with bushy brown hair. Born to non-magical parents, she excels in her studies at Hogwarts. Known for her quick wit and extensive knowledge, she is a key member of the trio.

Ron Weasley:

Harry’s loyal and humorous friend with red hair and freckles. Comes from a large, loving wizarding family and becomes one of Harry’s best friends. Often provides comic relief but is also brave and dependable.

Albus Dumbledore:

The wise and kind headmaster of Hogwarts with long silver hair and a beard. A powerful wizard who guides Harry and fights against Voldemort. Known for his wisdom, kindness, and twinkling blue eyes behind half-moon spectacles.

Lord Voldemort (Tom Riddle):

The main antagonist with a snake-like appearance and red eyes. Once a student at Hogwarts, he becomes the most dangerous dark wizard. Seeks to conquer the wizarding world and eliminate those he deems unworthy.

Severus Snape:

A complex and mysterious professor at Hogwarts with greasy black hair. Often misunderstood, his true loyalties and motivations are revealed gradually. Plays a crucial role in the series, with a deep and intricate backstory.
